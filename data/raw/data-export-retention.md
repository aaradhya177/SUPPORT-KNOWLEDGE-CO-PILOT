# Data Export and Retention

## Export options

Workspace administrators can export ticket data from Settings, Data Management, Exports. Standard exports include ticket fields, public replies, internal notes, tags, status history, requester information, and timestamps. Attachments are not included in every export format; administrators should choose the attachment-inclusive export option when they need files. Large exports are prepared asynchronously, and the administrator receives an email when the download is ready.

## Export timing and access

Small exports may finish within minutes, while large workspaces can take several hours. Export links expire after seven days for security. If a link expires, the administrator must create a new export. Only administrators with data management permission can start exports and download completed export files. Support agents cannot download customer exports on behalf of a workspace administrator.

## Retention policy

Northstar Desk retains active workspace ticket data for the life of the subscription unless a custom retention policy is configured. Enterprise customers may configure retention windows that automatically remove older closed tickets after the chosen period. Deleting a workspace starts a scheduled deletion process, but billing records and security logs may be retained separately for legal, tax, and abuse-prevention purposes.

## Compliance requests

For compliance or legal requests, support agents should collect the workspace slug, request type, desired date range, and administrator contact. Data deletion and legal hold requests should be escalated to the privacy operations team. Agents should not promise immediate deletion because retention obligations can vary by region, contract, and data category.
