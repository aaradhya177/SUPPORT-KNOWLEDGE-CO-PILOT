# Billing FAQ

## Invoice timing

Northstar Desk creates invoices on the first day of each billing cycle. Monthly plans are invoiced every month, while annual plans are invoiced once per year on the renewal date. The invoice includes active seats, plan charges, add-ons, credits, taxes, and any usage-based overages from the previous cycle. Customers can download invoices from Settings, Billing, Invoices. Billing contacts also receive a copy by email after the invoice is finalized.

## Updating payment methods

Administrators with billing permission can update the payment method from the billing settings page. The new card or bank account is validated immediately, but existing unpaid invoices are not always retried at the exact moment the payment method changes. Customers can select Pay now on an open invoice to trigger a manual attempt. If the payment still fails, they should confirm available funds, billing address accuracy, and any bank restrictions on recurring software purchases.

## Seat changes and prorations

When a customer adds seats during a billing cycle, Northstar Desk prorates the charge for the remaining time in that cycle. Removing seats lowers the next renewal amount, but it does not usually create an immediate refund unless the customer is on a contract that explicitly includes mid-cycle credits. Seat counts are based on active users, pending invitations, and service accounts. Deactivated users no longer count toward the next billing cycle after deactivation completes.

## Refund and tax questions

Refund eligibility depends on the plan, region, and contract terms. Support agents should collect the invoice number, account workspace, and reason for the request before escalating to billing operations. Tax exemption requests require a valid certificate and may take several business days to review. If tax was charged before approval, the billing team can determine whether a credit is available.
